MTRL to participate in the 2nd jDL Season

Following an outstanding 16-0 streak in the 5th Romanian group of the jDL Europe Starterf Division, Materiale went on to face Furys Dark Seer in the play-offs counting for the 2nd to 4th divisions. Unfortunately, the team lost a less than 20 minutes Blitzkrieg against the Danes, and therefore got sent to the 4th division.

This basically means that MTRL went up two divisions in the joinDOTA ladder, between seasons 1 and 2.

The 2nd jDL season begins on the 27th of April 2014. The team's progress can be tracked here.
